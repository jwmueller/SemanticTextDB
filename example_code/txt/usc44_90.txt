§ 1101. Printing and binding for the President
The Public Printer shall execute such printing and binding for the President as he may order and make requisition for.
(Pub. L. 90–620, Oct. 22, 1968, 82 Stat. 1261.)

Historical and Revision NotesBased on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 211 (Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 88, 28 Stat. 622).

Codification
Section constitutes part of section 88 of act Jan. 12, 1895. Remainder of section is classified to section 1713 of this title.
