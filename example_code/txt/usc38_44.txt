§ 313. Availability of appropriations(a) Funds appropriated to the Department may remain available until expended.

(b) Funds appropriated to the Department may not be used for a settlement of more than $1,000,000 on a construction contract unless—(1) the settlement is audited by an entity outside the Department for reasonableness and appropriateness of expenditures; and

(2) the settlement is provided for specifically in an appropriation law.


(Added Pub. L. 102–83, § 2(a), Aug. 6, 1991, 105 Stat. 383.)
