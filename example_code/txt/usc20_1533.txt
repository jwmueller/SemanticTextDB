§§ 6161, 6162. Omitted
Codification
Sections 6161 and 6162 were omitted pursuant to section 6251 of this title which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.
Section 6161, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 221, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 592, authorized grants.
Section 6162, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 222, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 592, related to requirements for grant recipients.
