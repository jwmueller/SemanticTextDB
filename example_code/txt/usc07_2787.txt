§ 8507. Miscellaneous matters(a) Availability of appropriated fundsAmounts appropriated under this chapter shall remain available until expended.


(b) Administrative expensesOf the amounts appropriated to carry out this chapter for a fiscal year, the Secretaries may expend not more than five percent to cover the administrative expenses necessary to carry out this chapter.


(Pub. L. 108–384, § 8, Oct. 30, 2004, 118 Stat. 2226.)
