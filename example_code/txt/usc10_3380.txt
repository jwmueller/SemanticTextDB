§ 10111. Air National Guard of the United States: composition
The Air National Guard of the United States is the reserve component of the Air Force that consists of—(1) federally recognized units and organizations of the Air National Guard; and

(2) members of the Air National Guard who are also Reserves of the Air Force.

(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1661(a)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2971.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 8077 of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 103–337, § 1661(a)(3)(A).
