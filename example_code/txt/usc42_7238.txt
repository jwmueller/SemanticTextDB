§ 14114. Scholarships(a) Period of awardScholarships awarded under this part shall be for a period of 1 academic year.


(b) Use of scholarshipsEach individual awarded a scholarship under this part may use the scholarship for educational expenses at an institution of higher education.


(Pub. L. 103–322, title XX, § 200205, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 2059.)
