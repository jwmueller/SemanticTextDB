§ 214. Previous laws unaffected
Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as modifying or revoking any of the provisions of sections 191 to 193 11 See References in Text note below. of this title.
(Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 74, § 13, 38 Stat. 822.)


References in TextSections 191 to 193 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1101(a)(1), Oct. 27, 1970, 84 Stat. 1291. See section 801 et seq. of this title.
