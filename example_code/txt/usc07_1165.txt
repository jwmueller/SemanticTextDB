§ 1636i. Termination of authority
The authority provided by this subchapter terminates on September 30, 2015.
(Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966, title II, § 260, as added Pub. L. 109–296, § 1(a), Oct. 5, 2006, 120 Stat. 1464; amended Pub. L. 111–239, § 2(a)(1), Sept. 27, 2010, 124 Stat. 2501.)

Amendments2010—Pub. L. 111–239 substituted “September 30, 2015” for “September 30, 2010”.
