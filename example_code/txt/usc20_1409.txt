§ 4801. Repealed. Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3975

Section, Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3202, Apr. 28, 1988, 102 Stat. 338, established Fund for the Improvement and Reform of Schools and Teaching.

Short TitlePub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3201, Apr. 28, 1988, 102 Stat. 338, provided that part B of title III of Pub. L. 100–297 (enacting this chapter) could be cited as the “Fund for the Improvement and Reform of Schools and Teaching Act”, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3975.
