§ 2611. Purposes
The purposes of this chapter are to encourage—(1) conservation of energy supplied by electric utilities;

(2) the optimization of the efficiency of use of facilities and resources by electric utilities; and

(3) equitable rates to electric consumers.

(Pub. L. 95–617, title I, § 101, Nov. 9, 1978, 92 Stat. 3120.)
