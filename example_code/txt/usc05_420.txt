§ 4801. Nonapplicability of chapter 47
Chapter 47 shall not apply to this chapter.
(Added Pub. L. 107–123, § 8(a), Jan. 16, 2002, 115 Stat. 2398.)

Effective DateChapter effective Oct. 1, 2001, see section 11 of Pub. L. 107–123, set out as an Effective Date of 2002 Amendment note under section 78ee of Title 15, Commerce and Trade.
