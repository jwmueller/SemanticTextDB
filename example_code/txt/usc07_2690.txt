§ 7813. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.


(b) Administrative expensesFunds appropriated under subsection (a) may not be used for the payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Board in administering a provision of an order.


(Pub. L. 106–387, § 1(a) [title XII, § 1214], Oct. 28, 2000, 114 Stat. 1549, 1549A–97.)
