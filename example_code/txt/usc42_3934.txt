§ 3797s–5. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2009 and 2010.


(b) Use of amountsOf the amount made available to carry out this subchapter in any fiscal year, not less than 5 percent shall be used for grants to Indian Tribes.


(Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2926, as added Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 113, Apr. 9, 2008, 122 Stat. 676.)

Construction of 2008 AmendmentFor construction of amendments by Pub. L. 110–199 and requirements for grants made under such amendments, see section 17504 of this title.
