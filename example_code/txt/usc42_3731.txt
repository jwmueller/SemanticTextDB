“SEC. 3404. MEMBERSHIP.“(a) [Repealed. Pub. L. 103–322, title XXVI, § 260002, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 2089.]

“(b) Compensation.—“(1) In general.—Members of the Commission shall receive no additional pay, allowances, or benefits by reason of service on the Commission.

“(2) Travel expenses.—Each member of the Commission shall receive travel expenses, including per diem in lieu of subsistence, in accordance with sections 5702 and 5703 of title 5, United States Code.
