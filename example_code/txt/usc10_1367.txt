§ 2200c. Centers of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education
In the selection of a recipient for the award of a scholarship or grant under this chapter, consideration shall be given to whether—(1) in the case of a scholarship, the institution at which the recipient pursues a degree is a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education; and

(2) in the case of a grant, the recipient is a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education.

(Added Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A], title IX, § 922(a)(1)], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–236.)
