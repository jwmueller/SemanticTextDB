§ 16321. Rare Isotope Accelerator(a) EstablishmentThe Secretary shall construct and operate a Rare Isotope Accelerator. The Secretary shall commence construction no later than September 30, 2008.


(b) Authorization of appropriationsThere are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary such sums as may be necessary to carry out this section. The Secretary shall not spend more than $1,100,000,000 in Federal funds for all activities associated with the Rare Isotope Accelerator, prior to operation of the Accelerator.


(Pub. L. 109–58, title IX, § 981, Aug. 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 907.)
