§ 3351. Definitions
As used in this subpart:(1) Canadian articleThe term “Canadian article” means an article that—(A) is an originating good under chapter 4 of the Agreement; and

(B) qualifies under the Agreement to be marked as a good of Canada.


(2) Mexican articleThe term “Mexican article” means an article that—(A) is an originating good under chapter 4 of the Agreement; and

(B) qualifies under the Agreement to be marked as a good of Mexico.


(Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 301, Dec. 8, 1993, 107 Stat. 2100.)
