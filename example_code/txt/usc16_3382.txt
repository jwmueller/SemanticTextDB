“SEC. 9. REPORT.“On the expiration of the third fiscal year for which any amounts are made available to carry out this Act, the Secretary shall, after partnering with local governmental entities and the States in the Pacific Ocean drainage area, submit to Congress a report describing—“(1) the projects that have been completed under this Act;

“(2) the projects that will be completed with amounts made available under this Act during the remaining fiscal years for which amounts are authorized to be appropriated under section 10; and

“(3) recommended changes to the Program as a result of projects that have been carried out under this Act.
