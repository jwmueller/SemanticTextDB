§ 80306. Restrictions(a) Profit.—The corporation may not operate for profit.

(b) Political Activities.—The corporation shall be nonpolitical and nonsectarian.

(Pub. L. 105–225, Aug. 12, 1998, 112 Stat. 1362.)
