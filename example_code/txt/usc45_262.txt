§ 1012. Publications and reports
Within 45 days after May 30, 1980, the Board shall publish, and make available for distribution by the Rock Island Railroad to all eligible employees, a document which describes in detail the rights of such employees under sections 1005, 1006,11 See References in Text note below. 1007, and 1014 of this title.
(Pub. L. 96–254, title I, § 115, May 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 405.)


References in TextSection 1006 of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1144(b), Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 669.
