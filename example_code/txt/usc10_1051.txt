§ 1436a. Coverage paid up at 30 years and age 70
Effective October 1, 2008, a reduction under this subchapter in the retired or retainer pay of a person electing an annuity under this subchapter may not be made for any month after the later of—(1) the month that is the 360th month for which that person’s retired or retainer pay is reduced pursuant to such an election; and

(2) the month during which that person attains 70 years of age.

(Added Pub. L. 106–65, div. A, title VI, § 655(a), Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 667.)
