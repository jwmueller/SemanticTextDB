§ 7201. Definitions
In this chapter:(1) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.


(2) SystemThe term “system” means the National Landscape Conservation System established by section 7202(a) of this title.


(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2001, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1094.)
