§§ 782, 783. Repealed. Pub. L. 87–558, § 1, July 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 246

Section 782, R.S. § 2442; act July 3, 1930, ch. 863, § 2, 46 Stat. 1016, required Secretary of the Interior to prescribe regulations to carry the provisions of section 829 of this title into effect.


Section 783, act Dec. 13, 1894, ch. 3, 28 Stat. 594, related to bounty warrants and indemnity certificate receivable in payment for lands.
