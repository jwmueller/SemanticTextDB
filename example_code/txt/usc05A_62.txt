§ 408. Reports to Congress
The Director shall, no later than April 30 of each year in which the second session of a Congress begins, submit to the Congress a report containing—(1) a summary of the actions taken by the Director during a 2-year period ending on December 31 of the preceding year in order to carry out the Director’s functions and responsibilities under this title; and

(2) such other information as the Director may consider appropriate.

(Pub. L. 95–521, title IV, § 408, as added Pub. L. 100–598, § 4, Nov. 3, 1988, 102 Stat. 3031; amended Pub. L. 104–179, § 4(b)(2)(B), Aug. 6, 1996, 110 Stat. 1567.)

Amendments1996—Pub. L. 104–179 substituted “April 30” for “March 31” in introductory provisions.
