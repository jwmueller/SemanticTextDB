§ 6841. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalIn addition to any other funds authorized to be appropriated for multilateral or bilateral programs related to HIV/AIDS or economic development, there is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of the Treasury $150,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2001 and 2002 for payment to the Trust Fund.


(b) Allocation of fundsOf the amounts authorized to be appropriated by subsection (a) of this section for the fiscal years 2001 and 2002, $50,000,000 are authorized to be available each such fiscal year only for programs that benefit orphans.


(Pub. L. 106–264, title I, § 141, Aug. 19, 2000, 114 Stat. 758.)
