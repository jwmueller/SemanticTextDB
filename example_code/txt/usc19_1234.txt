“SEC. 301. DEFINITIONS.“In this title:“(1) Omani article.—The term ‘Omani article’ means an article that—“(A) qualifies as an originating good under section 202(b); or

“(B) receives preferential tariff treatment under paragraphs 8 through 11 of article 3.2 of the Agreement.


“(2) Omani textile or apparel article.—The term ‘Omani textile or apparel article’ means an article that—“(A) is listed in the Annex to the Agreement on Textiles and Clothing referred to in section 101(d)(4) of the Uruguay Round Agreements Act (19 U.S.C. 3511(d)(4)); and

“(B) is an Omani article.


“(3) Commission.—The term ‘Commission’ means the United States International Trade Commission.
