§ 144. Copies of Statutes at Large
Ten of the copies of the Statutes at Large, published by Little, Brown &amp; Co., which were deposited in the Library prior to February 5, 1859, shall be retained by the Librarian for the use of the justices of the Supreme Court, during the terms of court.
(R.S. § 96.)

Codification
R.S. § 96 derived from act Feb. 5, 1859, ch. 22, § 11, 11 Stat. 381.
