§ 14115. Eligibility(a) ScholarshipsA person shall be eligible to receive a scholarship under this part if the person has been employed in law enforcement for the 2-year period immediately preceding the date on which assistance is sought.


(b) Ineligibility for student employmentA person who has been employed as a law enforcement officer is ineligible to participate in a student employment program carried out under this part.


(Pub. L. 103–322, title XX, § 200206, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 2059.)
