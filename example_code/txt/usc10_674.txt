§ 949n. Military commission to announce action
A military commission under this chapter shall announce its findings and sentence to the parties as soon as determined.
(Added Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title XVIII, § 1802, Oct. 28, 2009, 123 Stat. 2590.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 949n, added Pub. L. 109–366, § 3(a)(1), Oct. 17, 2006, 120 Stat. 2617, required a military commission to announce its findings and sentence as soon as determined, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by Pub. L. 111–84.
