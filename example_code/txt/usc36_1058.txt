§ 220101. Organization(a) Federal Charter.—United Service Organizations, Incorporated (in this chapter, the “corporation”), is a federally chartered corporation.

(b) Perpetual Existence.—Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.

(Pub. L. 105–225, Aug. 12, 1998, 112 Stat. 1459.)
