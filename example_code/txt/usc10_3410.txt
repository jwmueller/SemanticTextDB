§ 10209. Regular and reserve components: discrimination prohibited
Laws applying to both Regulars and Reserves shall be administered without discrimination—(1) among Regulars;

(2) among Reserves; and

(3) between Regulars and Reserves.

(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1661(a)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2978.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 277 of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 103–337, § 1661(a)(2)(A).
