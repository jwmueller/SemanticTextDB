§ 4710. Collection and compilation of information
The Fund shall—(1) collect and compile information pertinent to community development financial institutions that will assist in creating, developing, expanding, and preserving such institutions; and

(2) make such information available to promote the purposes of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 103–325, title I, § 111, Sept. 23, 1994, 108 Stat. 2177.)
