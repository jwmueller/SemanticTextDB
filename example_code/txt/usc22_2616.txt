§ 5301. Short title
This subchapter may be cited as the “Exchange Rates and International Economic Policy Coordination Act of 1988”.
(Pub. L. 100–418, title III, § 3001, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1372.)


References in TextThis subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this subtitle”, meaning subtitle A (§§ 3001–3006) of title III of Pub. L. 100–418, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1372, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of subtitle A to the Code, see Tables.
