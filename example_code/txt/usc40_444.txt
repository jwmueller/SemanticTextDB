§ 17706. Gross proceeds as miscellaneous receipts in the Treasury
The gross proceeds of each transfer shall be deposited in the Treasury as miscellaneous receipts.
(Pub. L. 107–217, Aug. 21, 2002, 116 Stat. 1288.)
