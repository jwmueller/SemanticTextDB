“SEC. 3202. GREAT LAKES SHORELINE MAPPING PLAN.“(a) Preparation of Plan.—Not later than nine months after the date of the enactment of this subtitle [Dec. 29, 1987], the Director, in consultation with the Director of the United States Geological Survey, shall submit to the Congress a plan for preparing maps of the shoreline of the Great Lakes under section 3203.

“(b) Content of Plan.—A plan prepared under paragraph (1) shall include—“(1) a work proposal and a division of responsibilities between the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and the United States Geological Survey;

“(2) a time schedule for completion of maps;

“(3) recommendation of funding needed for preparing the maps; and

“(4) an area mapping schedule, with first priority given to shoreline areas subject to a high risk of erosion or flooding.
