§ 11272. Dissemination
The Administrator of the Centers for Medicare &amp; Medicaid Services shall disseminate the results of research conducted under this part to appropriate professional entities and to the public.
(Pub. L. 99–660, title IX, § 938, formerly § 949B, Nov. 14, 1986, 100 Stat. 3812; renumbered § 938, Pub. L. 100–607, title I, § 142(c)(2)(B), Nov. 4, 1988, 102 Stat. 3057; amended Pub. L. 108–173, title IX, § 900(e)(5)(C), Dec. 8, 2003, 117 Stat. 2373.)

Amendments2003—Pub. L. 108–173 substituted “Centers for Medicare &amp; Medicaid Services” for “Health Care Financing Administration”.
