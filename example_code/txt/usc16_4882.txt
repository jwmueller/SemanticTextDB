§ 5901. “Secretary” defined
As used in this chapter, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior, except as otherwise specifically provided.
(Pub. L. 105–391, § 2, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3498.)


References in TextThis chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning Pub. L. 105–391, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3497, known as the National Parks Omnibus Management Act of 1998. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.
