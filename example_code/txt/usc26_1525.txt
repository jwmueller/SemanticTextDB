§ 5811. Transfer tax(a) RateThere shall be levied, collected, and paid on firearms transferred a tax at the rate of $200 for each firearm transferred, except, the transfer tax on any firearm classified as any other weapon under section 5845(e) shall be at the rate of $5 for each such firearm transferred.


(b) By whom paidThe tax imposed by subsection (a) of this section shall be paid by the transferor.


(c) PaymentThe tax imposed by subsection (a) of this section shall be payable by the appropriate stamps prescribed for payment by the Secretary.
