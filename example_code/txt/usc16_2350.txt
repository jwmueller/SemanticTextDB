§ 460zzz–5. Management plan(a) In generalNot later than 3 years after March 30, 2009, the Secretary shall develop a comprehensive management plan for the long-term protection and management of the Conservation Area.


(b) PurposesThe management plan shall—(1) describe the appropriate uses and management of the Conservation Area;

(2) be developed with extensive public input;

(3) take into consideration any information developed in studies of the land within the Conservation Area; and

(4) include a comprehensive travel management plan.


(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2406, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1107.)
