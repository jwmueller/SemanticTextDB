§ 451a. Harbors subject to this subchapter
The following harbors shall be subject to this subchapter:(1) The harbor of New York.

(2) The harbor of Hampton Roads.

(3) The harbor of Baltimore.

(June 29, 1888, ch. 496, § 6, 25 Stat. 210; Pub. L. 85–802, § 1(7), Aug. 28, 1958, 72 Stat. 970.)

Amendments1958—Pub. L. 85–802 substituted provisions making harbors of New York, Hampton Roads, and Baltimore subject to this subchapter for appropriation provisions.

Effective Date of 1958 AmendmentAmendment by Pub. L. 85–802 effective on sixtieth day after Aug. 28, 1958, see section 2 of Pub. L. 85–802, set out as a note under section 441 of this title.
