“SEC. 402. REQUIRED SUBMISSION.“(a) In General.—The owner of each covered multifamily housing property, and the owner of each covered multifamily housing property for the elderly, shall submit to the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development a comprehensive needs assessment of the property under this title. The assessment shall be prepared by an entity that does not have an identity of interest with the owner.

“(b) Timing.—To ensure that assessments for all covered multifamily housing properties will be submitted on or before the conclusion of fiscal year 1997, the Secretary shall require the owners of such properties, including covered multifamily housing properties for the elderly, to submit the assessments for the properties in accordance with the following schedule:“(1) For fiscal year 1994, 10 percent of the aggregate number of such properties.

“(2) For each of fiscal years 1995, 1996, and 1997, an additional 30 percent of the aggregate number of such properties.
