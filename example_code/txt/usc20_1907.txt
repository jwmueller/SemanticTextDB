§ 7265d. Availability of funds
If sufficient funds are made available under section 7241 of this title to carry out this subpart for a fiscal year, the Secretary shall make available, to support activities described in section 7265b(b) of this title, the following amounts:(1) Not less than $2,000,000 each to—(A) the New Bedford Whaling Museum, in partnership with the New Bedford Oceanarium, in Massachusetts;

(B) the Inupiat Heritage Center in Alaska; and

(C) the Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians in Choctaw, Mississippi.


(2) For the New Trade Winds project, not less than $1,000,000 each to—(A) the Alaska Native Heritage Center in Alaska;

(B) the Bishop Museum in Hawaii; and

(C) the Peabody-Essex Museum in Massachusetts.


(3) For internship and apprenticeship programs (including the Museum Action Corps of the Peabody-Essex Museum), not less than $1,000,000 each to—(A) the Alaska Native Heritage Center in Alaska;

(B) the Bishop Museum in Hawaii; and

(C) the Peabody-Essex Museum in Massachusetts.
