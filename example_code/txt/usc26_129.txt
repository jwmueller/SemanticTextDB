§ 85. Unemployment compensation(a) General ruleIn the case of an individual, gross income includes unemployment compensation.


(b) Unemployment compensation definedFor purposes of this section, the term “unemployment compensation” means any amount received under a law of the United States or of a State which is in the nature of unemployment compensation.


(c) Special rule for 2009In the case of any taxable year beginning in 2009, gross income shall not include so much of the unemployment compensation received by an individual as does not exceed $2,400.
