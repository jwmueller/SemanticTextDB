§ 7181. Short title
This part may be cited as the “Pro-Children Act of 2001”.
(Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4301, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1773.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 4301 of Pub. L. 89–10 was classified to section 3081 of this title, prior to the general amendment of Pub. L. 89–10 by Pub. L. 103–382.
