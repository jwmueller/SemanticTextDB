§ 103. Boundary Line
In this title, the term “Boundary Line” means a line established under section 2(b) of the Act of February 19, 1895 (33 U.S.C. 151).11 So in original. Probably should be “(33 U.S.C. 151(b)).”.
(Pub. L. 109–304, § 4, Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1486.)
