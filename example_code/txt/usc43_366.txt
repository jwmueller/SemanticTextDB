§ 390h–39. City of Corona Water Utility, California, water recycling and reuse project(a) AuthorizationThe Secretary, in cooperation with the City of Corona Water Utility, California, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and construction of, and land acquisition for, a project to reclaim and reuse wastewater, including degraded groundwaters, within and outside of the service area of the City of Corona Water Utility, California.


(b) Cost shareThe Federal share of the cost of the project authorized by this section shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost of the project.


(c) LimitationThe Secretary shall not provide funds for the operation and maintenance of the project authorized by this section.


(Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1656, as added Pub. L. 111–11, title IX, § 9114(a), Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1320.)
