§ 5504. Officers of Senate
Section 5503 of this title shall not apply to officers of the Senate.
(Pub. L. 95–521, title VII, § 714(d), Oct. 26, 1978, 92 Stat. 1884.)

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 118a of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Effective DateSection effective Jan. 3, 1979, see section 717 of Pub. L. 95–521, set out as a note under section 288 of this title.
