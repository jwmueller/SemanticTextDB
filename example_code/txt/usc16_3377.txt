“SEC. 4. PARTICIPATION IN THE PROGRAM.“(a) Non-Federal.—“(1) In general.—Non-Federal participation in the Program shall be voluntary.

“(2) Federal action.—The Secretary shall take no action that would result in any non-Federal entity being held financially responsible for any action under the Program, unless the entity applies to participate in the Program.


“(b) Federal.—Development and implementation of projects under the Program on land or facilities owned by the United States shall be nonreimbursable Federal expenditures.
