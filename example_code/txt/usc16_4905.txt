§ 5962. Use of nonmonetary consideration in concessions contracts
Section 1302 of title 40, relating to the leasing of buildings and properties of the United States, shall not apply to contracts awarded by the Secretary pursuant to this subchapter.
(Pub. L. 105–391, title IV, § 413, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3515.)

Codification
“Section 1302 of title 40” substituted in text for “Section 321 of the Act of June 30, 1932 (40 U.S.C. 303b)” on authority of Pub. L. 107–217, § 5(c), Aug. 21, 2002, 116 Stat. 1303, the first section of which enacted Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.
