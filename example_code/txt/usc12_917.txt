§§ 1749bbb–3 to 1749bbb–6a. Omitted
Codification
Sections 1749bbb–3 to 1749bbb–6a, comprising part A of this subchapter, terminated on Sept. 30, 1985, pursuant to former section 1749bbb(b) of this title.
