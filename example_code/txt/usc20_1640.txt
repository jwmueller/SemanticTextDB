§ 6553. Authorization of appropriations
For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $125,000,000 for fiscal year 2002 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 5 succeeding fiscal years, of which—(1) 10 percent shall be available to carry out subpart 1 of this part for each fiscal year; and

(2) 90 percent shall be available to carry out subpart 2 of this part for each fiscal year.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1803, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1610.)
