§ 8302. Definitions
In this chapter:(1) VfP OfficeThe term “VfP Office” means the Office of Volunteers for Prosperity of the United States Agency for International Development.


(2) VfP ProgramThe term “VfP Program” means the Volunteers for Prosperity Program established through Executive Order 13317.


(3) VfPServeThe term “VfPServe” means a program established by the VfP Office, in cooperation with the USA Freedom Corps, to provide eligible skilled professionals with fixed amount stipends to offset the travel and living costs of volunteering abroad.


(Pub. L. 111–13, title V, § 5102, Apr. 21, 2009, 123 Stat. 1598.)


References in TextExecutive Order 13317, referred to in par. (2), is set out as a note under section 8301 of this title.
