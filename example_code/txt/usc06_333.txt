§ 925. Border Patrol unit for United States Virgin Islands(a) In generalThe Secretary may establish at least 1 Border Patrol unit for the United States Virgin Islands.


(b) ReportNot later than 180 days after October 13, 2006, the Secretary shall submit a report to the appropriate congressional committees that includes the schedule, if any, for carrying out subsection (a).


(Pub. L. 109–347, title I, § 126, Oct. 13, 2006, 120 Stat. 1900.)
