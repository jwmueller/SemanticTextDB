Section, added Pub. L. 89–44, title II, § 202(b), June 21, 1965, 79 Stat. 137; amended Pub. L. 91–258, title II, § 207(b), May 21, 1970, 84 Stat. 248; Pub. Effective Date of RepealRepeal applicable with respect to articles sold after Jan. 6, 1983, see section 515(c) of Pub. L. 97–424, set out as an Effective Date of 1983 Amendment note under section 34 of this title.
