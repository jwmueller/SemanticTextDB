§ 152611. Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated to the foundation for carrying out the purposes of this chapter—(1) $5,000,000 for fiscal year 2003; and

(2) such sums as may be necessary for each succeeding fiscal year.

(Added Pub. L. 107–252, title VI, § 601(a), Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1721.)
