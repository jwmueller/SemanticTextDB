§ 11335. Annual report
The National Board shall transmit to the Congress an annual report covering each year in which it conducts activities with funds made available under this subchapter.
(Pub. L. 100–77, title III, § 305, July 22, 1987, 101 Stat. 491.)

Termination of Reporting RequirementsFor termination, effective May 15, 2000, of provisions of law requiring submittal to Congress of any annual, semiannual, or other regular periodic report listed in House Document No. 103–7 (in which a report required under this section is listed in the 4th item on page 169), see section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66, as amended, set out as a note under section 1113 of Title 31, Money and Finance.
