§ 1541. Establishment of Advisory Commission(a) EstablishmentThere is established a commission to be known as the “Advisory Commission on Drug-Free Communities”.


(b) PurposeThe Advisory Commission shall advise, consult with, and make recommendations to the Director concerning matters related to the activities carried out under the Program.


(Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1041, as added Pub. L. 105–20, § 2(a)(2), June 27, 1997, 111 Stat. 231.)
