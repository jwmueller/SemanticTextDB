“SEC. 8. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.“(a) Section 3.—There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 3 of this Act $5,000,000 per year for fiscal years 1997 through 2013. Of these amounts, up to $1,000,000 in each fiscal year may be awarded to institutions of higher education, including United States-Mexico binational research foundations and interuniversity research programs established by the two countries, for research grants without any cost-sharing requirement.

“(b) Section 4.—There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 4 of this Act $3,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2012 through 2013.
