§ 21053. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated for grants under this subpart $10,000,000 for fiscal year 2003.


(b) Availability of fundsAmounts appropriated pursuant to the authorization under this section shall remain available, without fiscal year limitation, until expended.


(Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 283, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1702.)

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 15453 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
