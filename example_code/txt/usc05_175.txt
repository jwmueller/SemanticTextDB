§ 2101a. The Senior Executive Service
The “Senior Executive Service” consists of Senior Executive Service positions (as defined in section 3132(a)(2) of this title).
(Added Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, § 401(a), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1154.)

Effective DateSection effective 9 months after Oct. 13, 1978, and congressional review of provisions of sections 401 through 412 of Pub. L. 95–454, see section 415 of Pub. L. 95–454, set out as a note under section 3131 of this title.
