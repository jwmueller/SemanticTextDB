§ 2434. Termination of authority(a) In generalSubject to subsection (b), the authority of the Secretary to carry out this subchapter terminates on the date that is 10 years after December 22, 2006.


(b) ExceptionThe termination of authority under subsection (a) shall have no effect on—(1) any loans guaranteed by the United States under this subchapter; or

(2) the administration of any loan guaranteed under this subchapter before the effective date of the termination of authority.


(Pub. L. 109–451, title II, § 215, Dec. 22, 2006, 120 Stat. 3361.)
