§ 8145. Administration
The Secretary of Labor shall administer, and decide all questions arising under, this subchapter. He may—(1) appoint employees to administer this subchapter; and

(2) delegate to any employee of the Department of Labor any of the powers conferred on him by this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 553.)
