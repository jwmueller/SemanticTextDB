§ 1909. Fiscal year 1992 funding(a) Authorization of appropriations to FundThere is hereby authorized to be appropriated to the Fund for fiscal year 1992 the sum of $150,000,000.


(b) Authorization of obligations from FundDuring fiscal year 1992, there may be obligated from the Fund such amounts as may be provided in appropriations Acts, not to exceed $35,000,000. Amounts made available for obligation from the Fund for fiscal year 1992 shall remain available until expended.


(Pub. L. 102–183, title VIII, § 809, Dec. 4, 1991, 105 Stat. 1277.)
