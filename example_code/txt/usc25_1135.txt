“SEC. 4. REMOVAL OF RESTRICTION.“(a) In General.—[Amended section 953(b) of this title.]

“(b) Applicability.—The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply as if this section had been enacted on March 31, 1983.

“(c) Subsequent Distributions.—Any per capita distribution of tribal revenues of the Band made after the date of enactment of this Act [Oct. 30, 1998] shall be made to all members of the Band in equal amounts.”
