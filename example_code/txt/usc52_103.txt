§ 21022. Amount of payment(a) In generalThe amount of a payment made to an eligible State or an eligible unit of local government for a year under this subpart shall be determined by the Secretary.


(b) Continuing availability of funds after appropriationA payment made to an eligible State or eligible unit of local government under this subpart shall be available without fiscal year limitation.


(Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 262, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1698.)

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 15422 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
