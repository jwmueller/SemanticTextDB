§ 410eee–1. Definitions
As used in this subchapter:(1) Historical parkThe term “historical park” means the Adams National Historical Park established in section 410eee–2 of this title.


(2) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.


(Pub. L. 105–342, § 3, Nov. 2, 1998, 112 Stat. 3201.)
