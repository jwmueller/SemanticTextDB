§ 61g. Transferred
Codification
Section 61g was editorially reclassified as section 6151 of this title.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 61g, acts Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 568, 69 Stat. 502; June 27, 1956, ch. 453, § 101, 70 Stat. 357, prescribed the gross annual compensation of Secretaries of Senate Majority and Minority.
