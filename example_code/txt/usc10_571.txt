§ 877. Art. 77. Principals
Any person punishable under this chapter who—(1) commits an offense punishable by this chapter, or aids, abets, counsels, commands, or procures its commission; or

(2) causes an act to be done which if directly performed by him would be punishable by this chapter;


is a principal.
(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 65.)
