§ 7223i. Definitions
In this subpart:(1) Charter schoolThe term “charter school” has the meaning given such term in section 7221i of this title.


(2) Eligible entityThe term “eligible entity” means—(A) a public entity, such as a State or local governmental entity;

(B) a private nonprofit entity; or

(C) a consortium of entities described in subparagraphs (A) and (B).


(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5230, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1803.)
