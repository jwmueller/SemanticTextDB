§ 1352. Alternative tax on qualifying shipping activities
In the case of an electing corporation, the tax imposed by section 11 shall be the amount equal to the sum of—(1) the tax imposed by section 11 determined after the application of this subchapter, and

(2) a tax equal to—(A) the highest rate of tax specified in section 11, multiplied by

(B) the notional shipping income for the taxable year.


(Added Pub. L. 108–357, title II, § 248(a), Oct. 22, 2004, 118 Stat. 1450.)

Effective DateSubchapter applicable to taxable years beginning after Oct. 22, 2004, see section 248(c) of Pub. L. 108–357, set out as an Effective Date of 2004 Amendments note under section 56 of this title.
