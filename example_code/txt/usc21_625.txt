§ 1548. Termination
The Advisory Commission shall terminate at the end of fiscal year 2007.
(Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1048, as added Pub. L. 105–20, § 2(a)(2), June 27, 1997, 111 Stat. 234; amended Pub. L. 107–82, § 3, Dec. 14, 2001, 115 Stat. 820.)

Amendments2001—Pub. L. 107–82 substituted “2007” for “2002”.
