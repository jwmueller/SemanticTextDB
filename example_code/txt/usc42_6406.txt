§ 11294. Authorization of appropriations(a) To carry out sections 11291 and 11293 of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated $1,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1988 through 1991.

(b) There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1992 through 1996, to carry out section 11292 of this title.

(Pub. L. 99–660, title IX, § 964, Nov. 14, 1986, 100 Stat. 3815; Pub. L. 102–507, § 8(b), Oct. 24, 1992, 106 Stat. 3287.)

Amendments1992—Pub. L. 102–507 designated existing provisions as subsec. (a), substituted “sections 11291 and 11293 of this title” for “this subchapter”, and added subsec. (b).
