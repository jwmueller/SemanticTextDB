§ 16013. Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subtitle and the amendments made by this subtitle.
(Pub. L. 109–58, title VI, § 636, Aug. 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 791.)


References in TextThis subtitle, referred to in text, is subtitle B (§§ 621–639) of title VI of Pub. L. 109–58, Aug. 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 782, which enacted this part and sections 2015b, 2210c, and 5853 of this title, amended sections 2133, 2135, 2158, 2160d, 2201, 2210a, 2214, 2297h–8, and 5851 of this title, repealed section 2213 of this title, and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 2158 and 2214 of this title. For complete classification of this subtitle to the Code, see Tables.
