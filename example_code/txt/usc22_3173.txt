§ 8004. United States compliance with its nuclear nonproliferation treaty obligations
Nothing in this chapter constitutes authority for any action in violation of an obligation of the United States under the NPT.
(Pub. L. 109–401, title I, § 105, Dec. 18, 2006, 120 Stat. 2738.)


References in TextThis chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of Pub. L. 109–401, Dec. 18, 2006, 120 Stat. 2726, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 8001 of this title and Tables.
