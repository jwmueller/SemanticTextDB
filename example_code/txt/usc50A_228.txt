§ 1112. Repealed. July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1, 61 Stat. 449

Section, act June 11, 1942, ch. 404, § 12, 56 Stat. 357, related to suspension of the anti-trust laws.

Outstanding CertificatesAct July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1, 61 Stat. 449, which repealed this section, provided that outstanding certificates issued under this section should continue in effect for a period of six months from July 25, 1947, unless sooner revoked.
