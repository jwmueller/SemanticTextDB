§ 9242. Local application
Each eligible provider desiring a grant or contract under this part shall submit an application to the eligible agency containing such information and assurances as the eligible agency may require, including—(1) a description of how funds awarded under this part will be spent; and

(2) a description of any cooperative arrangements the eligible provider has with other agencies, institutions, or organizations for the delivery of adult education and literacy activities.

(Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 232, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1072.)
