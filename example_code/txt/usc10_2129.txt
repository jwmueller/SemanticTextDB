§ 3251. Definition
In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.
(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 177; Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title XII, § 1231(19)(A), Dec. 4, 1987, 101 Stat. 1161.)
