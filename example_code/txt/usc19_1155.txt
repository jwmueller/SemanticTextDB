“SEC. 301. DEFINITIONS.“In this title:“(1) Korean article.—The term ‘Korean article’ means an article that qualifies as an originating good under section 202(b).

“(2) Korean motor vehicle article.—The term ‘Korean motor vehicle article’ means a good provided for in heading 8703 or 8704 of the HTS that qualifies as an originating good under section 202(b).

“(3) Korean textile or apparel article.—The term ‘Korean textile or apparel article’ means a textile or apparel good (as defined in section 3(5)) that is a Korean article.
