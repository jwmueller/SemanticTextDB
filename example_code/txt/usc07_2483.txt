§ 7111. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.


(b) Administrative expensesFunds appropriated under subsection (a) shall not be available for payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Board in administering any provision of any order issued under this chapter.


(Pub. L. 103–407, § 12, Oct. 22, 1994, 108 Stat. 4226.)
