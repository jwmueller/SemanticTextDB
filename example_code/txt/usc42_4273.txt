§ 4854b. Authorization
Of the total amount approved in appropriation Acts under section 4852(o) 11 See References in Text note below. of this title, there shall be set aside to carry out this part $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, and $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1994.
(Pub. L. 102–550, title X, § 1053, Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 3926.)


References in TextSection 4852(o) of this title, referred to in text, was redesignated section 4852(p) of this title by Pub. L. 103–233, title III, § 305(a)(1), Apr. 11, 1994, 108 Stat. 370.
