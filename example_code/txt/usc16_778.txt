“SEC. 3. ACQUISITION OF LAND.“(a) In General.—The Secretary may accept from any willing donor the donation of any land or interest in land of the Subdivision.

“(b) Administration.—On acquisition of land or an interest in land under subsection (a), the Secretary shall—“(1) include the land or interest in the boundaries of the Park; and

“(2) administer the land or interest as part of the Park, in accordance with all applicable laws (including regulations).


“(c) Deadline for Acquisition.—It is the intent of Congress that the acquisition of land or an interest in land under subsection (a) be completed not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act [July 13, 2007].

“(d) Restriction on Transfer.—The Secretary shall not donate, sell, exchange, or otherwise transfer any land acquired under this section without express authorization from Congress.
