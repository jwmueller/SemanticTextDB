§ 16204. Regulations
This chapter shall be administered under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Defense.
(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1663(c)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 3007.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 2130(c) of this title, prior to amendment by Pub. L. 103–337, § 1663(c)(4)(A).

Effective DateSection effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337, set out as a note under section 10001 of this title.
