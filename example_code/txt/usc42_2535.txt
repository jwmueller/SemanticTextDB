“SEC. 10. TERMINATION; ACCESS TO INFORMATION.“(a) Termination.—The Commission shall terminate 30 days after submitting the report required by section 8.

“(b) Access to Information.—On or before the date of the termination of the Commission under subsection (a), the Commission shall provide to the National Science Foundation the information gathered by the Commission in the process of carrying out its duties under this Act. The National Science Foundation shall act as a central repository for such information and shall make such information available to the public, including making such information available through the Internet.
