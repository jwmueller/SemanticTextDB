§ 7231b. Program authorized
The Secretary, in accordance with this part, is authorized to award grants to eligible local educational agencies, and consortia of such agencies where appropriate, to carry out the purpose of this part for magnet schools that are—(1) part of an approved desegregation plan; and

(2) designed to bring students from different social, economic, ethnic, and racial backgrounds together.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5303, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1807.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 5303 of Pub. L. 89–10 was classified to section 7263 of this title, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–110.
