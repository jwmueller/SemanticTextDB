§ 2278b–10. Exemption from taxation(a) AssetsThe Financial Assistance Corporation, and the capital, reserves, and surplus thereof, and the income derived therefrom, shall be exempt from Federal, State, municipal, and local taxation, except taxes on real estate held by the Financial Assistance Corporation to the same extent, according to its value, as other similar property held by other persons is taxed.


(b) ObligationsThe notes, bonds, debentures, and other obligations issued by the Financial Assistance Corporation shall be accorded the same tax treatment as System-wide obligations.


(Pub. L. 92–181, title VI, § 6.30, as added Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 201, Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1604.)
