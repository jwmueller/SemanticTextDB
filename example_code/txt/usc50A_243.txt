§§ 1261, 1262. Repealed. July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1, 61 Stat. 449

Section 1261, acts May 2, 1941, ch. 84, § 2, 55 Stat. 148; June 16, 1942, ch. 416, 56 Stat. 370, authorized United States Maritime Commission to negotiate contracts without advertisement or bids.


Section 1262, acts May 2, 1941, ch. 84, § 3, 55 Stat. 149; June 16, 1942, ch. 416, 56 Stat. 370, authorized charter of Commission vessels to private operators for foreign trade until six months after the end of World War II.
