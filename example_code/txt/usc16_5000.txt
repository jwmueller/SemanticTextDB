§ 6707. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter $15,000,000 for each fiscal year.


(b) LimitationNo funds made available under subsection (a) shall be used to pay the costs of constructing any facilities.


(Pub. L. 108–317, § 8, Oct. 5, 2004, 118 Stat. 1210.)
