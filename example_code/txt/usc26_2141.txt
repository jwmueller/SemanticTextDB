§ 9013. Effective date of chapter
The provisions of this chapter shall take effect on January 1, 1973.
(Added Pub. L. 92–178, title VIII, § 801, Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 572.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 9021, added by Pub. L. 92–178, title VIII, § 801, Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 572, established Presidential Election Campaign Fund Advisory Board, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 93–443, title IV, § 408(c), Oct. 15, 1974, 88 Stat. 1297. For effective date of repeal see section 410(c)(1) of Pub. L. 93–443, set out as an Effective Date of 1974 Amendment note under section 30101 of Title 52, Voting and Elections.
