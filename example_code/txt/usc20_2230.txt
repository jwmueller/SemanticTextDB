§ 9601. Definitions
In this subchapter:(1) In generalThe terms “local educational agency” and “State educational agency” have the meanings given those terms in section 7801 of this title.


(2) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Education.


(Pub. L. 107–279, title II, § 202, Nov. 5, 2002, 116 Stat. 1975.)

Short TitleThis subchapter known as the “Educational Technical Assistance Act of 2002”, see Short Title note set out under section 9501 of this title.
