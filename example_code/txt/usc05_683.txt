“Sec. 101. For purposes of this title—“(1) the term ‘credit hours’ means any hours, within a flexible schedule established under this title, which are in excess of an employee’s basic work requirement and which the employee elects to work so as to vary the length of a workweek or a workday; and

“(2) the term ‘overtime hours’ means all hours in excess of 8 hours in a day or 40 hours in a week which are officially ordered in advance, but does not include credit hours.
