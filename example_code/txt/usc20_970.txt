§ 1466. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part (other than section 1462 of this title) such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2005 through 2010.


(b) ReservationFrom amounts appropriated under subsection (a) for fiscal year 2005, the Secretary shall reserve $1,000,000 to carry out the study authorized in section 1464(c) of this title. From amounts appropriated under subsection (a) for a succeeding fiscal year, the Secretary may reserve an additional amount to carry out such study if the Secretary determines the additional amount is necessary.


(Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 667, as added Pub. L. 108–446, title I, § 101, Dec. 3, 2004, 118 Stat. 2787.)
