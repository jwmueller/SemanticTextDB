§ 1042. Copy of certificate of service
A fee for a copy of a certificate showing service in the armed forces may not be charged to—(1) a person discharged or released from the armed forces honorably or under honorable conditions;

(2) the next of kin of the person; or

(3) a legal representative of the person.

(Added Pub. L. 97–258, § 2(b)(2)(B), Sept. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1052.)
