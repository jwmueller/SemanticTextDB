§ 6751. Short title
This part may be cited as the “Enhancing Education Through Technology Act of 2001”.
(Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2401, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title II, § 201, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1671.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 2401 of Pub. L. 89–10 was classified to section 6701 of this title, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–110.
