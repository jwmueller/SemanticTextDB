§ 20162. Definition of upper atmosphere
In this subchapter, the term “upper atmosphere” means that portion of the Earth’s sensible atmosphere above the troposphere.
(Pub. L. 111–314, § 3, Dec. 18, 2010, 124 Stat. 3354.)
