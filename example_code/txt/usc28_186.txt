§ 501. Executive department
The Department of Justice is an executive department of the United States at the seat of Government.
(Added Pub. L. 89–554, § 4(c), Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 611.)

L. 94–503, title II, § 204, Oct. 15, 1976, 90 Stat. 2427, provided that: “No sums shall be deemed to be authorized to be appropriated for any fiscal year beginning on or after October 1, 1978, for the Department of Justice (including any bureau, agency, or other similar subdivision thereof) except as specifically authorized by Act of Congress with respect to such fiscal year.
