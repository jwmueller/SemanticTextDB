§ 16212. High power density industry program(a) In generalThe Secretary shall establish a comprehensive research, development, demonstration, and commercial application to improve the energy efficiency of high power density facilities, including data centers, server farms, and telecommunications facilities.


(b) TechnologiesThe program shall consider technologies that provide significant improvement in thermal controls, metering, load management, peak load reduction, or the efficient cooling of electronics.


(Pub. L. 109–58, title IX, § 922, Aug. 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 864.)
