§ 410uu–2. Cooperative agreements
The Secretary may enter into a cooperative agreement with the Ohio Historical Society, the Archeological 11 So in original. Probably should be “Archaeological”. Conservancy, and other public and private entities for consultation and assistance in the interpretation and management of the park.
(Pub. L. 102–294, § 3, May 27, 1992, 106 Stat. 186.)
