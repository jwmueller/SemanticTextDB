“SEC. 301. DEFINITIONS.“In this title:“(1) Peruvian article.—The term ‘Peruvian article’ means an article that qualifies as an originating good under section 203(b).

“(2) Peruvian textile or apparel article.—The term ‘Peruvian textile or apparel article’ means a textile or apparel good (as defined in section 3(4)) that is a Peruvian article.
