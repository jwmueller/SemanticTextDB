§ 33. Transferred
Codification
Section 33 was editorially reclassified as section 6301 of this title.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 33, act Mar. 3, 1883, ch. 143, 22 Stat. 632, entitled Senators to receive their compensation monthly, from the beginning of their term, prior to repeal by section 112(b)(1) of Pub. L. 97–51.
