“SEC. 4. DIPLOMATIC REPRESENTATION.“Subject to the direction of the President and the Secretary of State, the Special Envoy is authorized to represent the United States in matters and cases relevant to religious freedom in the countries of the Near East and the countries of South Central Asia in—“(1) contacts with foreign governments, intergovernmental organizations, and specialized agencies of the United Nations, the Organization of Security and Cooperation in Europe, and other international organizations of which the United States is a member; and

“(2) multilateral conferences and meetings relevant to religious freedom in the countries of the Near East and the countries of South Central Asia.
