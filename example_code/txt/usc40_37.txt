§ 529. Annual executive agency reports on excess personal property(a) In General.—During the calendar quarter following the close of each fiscal year, each executive agency shall submit to the Administrator of General Services a report on personal property—(1) obtained as—(A) excess property; or

(B) personal property determined to be no longer required for the purpose of the appropriation used to make the purchase; and


(2) furnished within the United States to a recipient other than a federal agency.


(b) Required Information.—The report must set out the categories of equipment and show—(1) the acquisition cost of the property;

(2) the recipient of the property; and

(3) other information the Administrator may require.


(Pub. L. 107–217, Aug. 21, 2002, 116 Stat. 1086.)
