§ 903. Definitions
For the purposes of this subchapter—(1) The term “tribe” means the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin.

(2) The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(3) The term “Menominee Restoration Committee” means that committee of nine Menominee Indians who shall be elected pursuant to subsections (a) and (b) of section 903b of this title.

(Pub. L. 93–197, § 2, Dec. 22, 1973, 87 Stat. 770.)

Short TitlePub. L. 93–197, § 1, Dec. 22, 1973, 87 Stat. 770, provided: “That this Act [enacting this subchapter and repealing sections 891 to 902 of this title] may be cited as the ‘Menominee Restoration Act’.”
