§ 12376. Definitions
For purposes of this subchapter—(1) the term “Conference” means the 1993 White House Conference on Children, Youth, and Families; and

(2) the terms “child”, “youth”, and “young individual” means 11 So in original. Probably should be “mean”. an individual who is less than 21 years of age.

(Pub. L. 101–501, title IX, § 987, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1283.)
