§ 2278a–12. Termination
The Assistance Board and the authority provided to the Assistance Board by this part shall terminate on December 31, 1992.
(Pub. L. 92–181, title VI, § 6.12, as added Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 201, Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1594; amended Pub. L. 100–399, title II, § 201(m), Aug. 17, 1988, 102 Stat. 991.)

Amendments1988—Pub. L. 100–399 inserted “to the Assistance Board” after “provided”.

Effective Date of 1988 AmendmentAmendment by Pub. L. 100–399 effective as if enacted immediately after enactment of Pub. L. 100–233, which was approved Jan. 6, 1988, see section 1001(a) of Pub. L. 100–399, set out as a note under section 2002 of this title.
