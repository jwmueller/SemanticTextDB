§ 13861. Grant authorization(a) In generalThe Attorney General may make grants to State, Indian tribal, or local prosecutors for the purpose of supporting the creation or expansion of community-based justice programs.


(b) ConsultationThe Attorney General may consult with the Ounce of Prevention Council in making grants under subsection (a) of this section.


(Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31701, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 1890.)
