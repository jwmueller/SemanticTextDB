§ 3384. Regulations
The Secretary is authorized to issue regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.
(Added Pub. L. 92–297, § 3(a), May 16, 1972, 86 Stat. 143; amended Pub. L. 96–347, § 1(b), Sept. 12, 1980, 94 Stat. 1150.)
