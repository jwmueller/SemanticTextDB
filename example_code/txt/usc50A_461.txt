§ 2061. Short title
This Act [sections 2061 to 2170, 2171, and 2172 of this Appendix], divided into titles, may be cited as “the Defense Production Act of 1950”.
(Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, § 1, 64 Stat. 798.)
