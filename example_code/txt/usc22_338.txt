§ 283k. Authorization of appropriations(a) Capital stock of Inter-American Development BankThere is hereby authorized to be appropriated, without fiscal year limitation, for payment of the increased United States subscription to the capital stock of the Inter-American Development Bank, $411,760,000.


(b) Fund for Special Operations of the Inter-American Development BankThere is hereby authorized to be appropriated, for payment of the increased United States subscription to the Fund for Special Operations of the Inter-American Development Bank, $50,000,000.


(Pub. L. 88–259, § 2, Jan. 22, 1964, 78 Stat. 3.)

Codification
Section was not enacted as part of Pub. L. 86–147, Aug. 7, 1959, 73 Stat. 299, known as the Inter-American Development Bank Act, which comprises this subchapter.
