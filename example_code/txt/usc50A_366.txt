§ 1941b. Furnishing of information concerning facilities to Commission on request
The Commission shall be furnished upon its request all available information concerning the Government-owned rubber-producing facilities in the possession of any department, agency, officer, Government corporation, or instrumentality of the United States concerned with Government-owned rubber-producing facilities.
(Aug. 7, 1953, ch. 338, § 4, 67 Stat. 409.)
