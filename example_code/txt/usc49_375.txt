§ 15502. Authority for pipeline carriers to establish rates, classifications, rules, and practices
A pipeline carrier providing transportation or service subject to this part shall establish—(1) rates and classifications for transportation and service it may provide under this part; and

(2) rules and practices on matters related to that transportation or service.

(Added Pub. L. 104–88, title I, § 106(a), Dec. 29, 1995, 109 Stat. 923.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 10702 of this title prior to the general amendment of this subtitle by Pub. L. 104–88, § 102(a).
