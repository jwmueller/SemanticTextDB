§ 971k. Savings clause
Nothing in this chapter shall have the effect of diminishing the rights and obligations of any Nation under Article VIII(3) of the Convention.
(Pub. L. 94–70, § 12, as added Pub. L. 104–43, title III, § 308, Nov. 3, 1995, 109 Stat. 387; amended Pub. L. 105–384, title II, § 202(b)(1)(E), Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3453.)

Amendments1998—Pub. L. 105–384 made technical amendment to style of heading and section designation in original act.
