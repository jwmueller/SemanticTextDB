§ 3907. Authorization of appropriations(a) General authorizationThere are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.


(b) CoordinationThe activities required to be performed by the Director under this chapter shall be coordinated with activities related to solid waste and sanitation facilities funded pursuant to other authorizations.


(Pub. L. 103–399, § 8, Oct. 22, 1994, 108 Stat. 4168.)
