§ 21042. Report(a) In generalEach entity which receives a grant under this subpart shall submit to the Commission a report describing the activities carried out with the funds provided under the grant.


(b) DeadlineAn entity shall submit a report required under subsection (a) not later than 60 days after the end of the fiscal year for which the entity received the grant which is the subject of the report.


(Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 272, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1700.)

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 15442 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
