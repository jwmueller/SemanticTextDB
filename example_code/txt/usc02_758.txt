§ 438. Transferred
Codification
Section 438 was editorially reclassified as section 30111 of Title 52, Voting and Elections.

Annual Reports for Calendar Years Beginning After Dec. 31, 1972Pub. L. 93–443, title II, § 209(a)(2), Oct. 15, 1974, 88 Stat. 1287, provided that notwithstanding a provision of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 requiring an annual report, no such annual report was required with respect to any calendar year beginning after December 31, 1972.
