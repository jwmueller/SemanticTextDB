§ 51. Short title
This chapter shall be known by the short title of “United States Cotton Standards Act.”
(Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 288, § 1, 42 Stat. 1517.)

Effective DateAct Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 288, § 14, 42 Stat. 1520, provided: “That this Act [enacting this chapter] shall become effective on and after August 1, 1923.”
