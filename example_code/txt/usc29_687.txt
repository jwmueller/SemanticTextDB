§ 2634. Compensation(a) PayMembers of the Commission shall serve without compensation.


(b) Travel expensesMembers of the Commission shall be allowed reasonable travel expenses, including a per diem allowance, in accordance with section 5703 of title 5 when performing duties of the Commission.


(Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 304, Feb. 5, 1993, 107 Stat. 25.)
