§ 11924. Definitions
For purposes of this subchapter:(1) Controlled substanceThe term “controlled substance” has the meaning given such term in section 802 of title 21.


(2) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development.


(Pub. L. 100–690, title V, § 5145, Nov. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 4304.)
