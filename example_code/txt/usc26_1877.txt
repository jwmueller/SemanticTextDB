§ 6720C. Penalty for failure to notify health plan of cessation of eligibility for COBRA premium assistance(a) In generalAny person required to notify a group health plan under section 3001(a)(2)(C) of title III of division B of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 who fails to make such a notification at such time and in such manner as the Secretary of Labor may require shall pay a penalty of 110 percent of the premium reduction provided under such section after termination of eligibility under such subsection.


(b) Reasonable cause exceptionNo penalty shall be imposed under subsection (a) with respect to any failure if it is shown that such failure is due to reasonable cause and not to willful neglect.


(Added Pub. L. 111–5, div. B, title III, § 3001(a)(13)(A), Feb. 17, 2009, 123 Stat. 464; amended Pub. L. 111–144, § 3(b)(5)(D), Mar. 2, 2010, 124 Stat. 45.)
