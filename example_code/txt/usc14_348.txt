§ 720. Definitions
As used in this subchapter—(1) “Reserve officer” means a commissioned officer in the Reserve, except an officer excluded by section 721 of this title or a commissioned warrant officer; and

(2) “discharged” means released from an appointment as a Reserve officer.

(Added Pub. L. 96–322, § 1, Aug. 4, 1980, 94 Stat. 1007.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 770 of this title prior to the complete revision of this chapter by Pub. L. 96–322.
