§ 8955. Information to individuals eligible to enroll(a) The qualified companies 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by a comma. at the direction and with the approval of the Office, shall make available to each individual eligible to enroll in a dental benefits plan information on services and benefits (including maximums, limitations, and exclusions), that the Office considers necessary to enable the individual to make an informed decision about electing coverage.

(b) The Office shall make available to each individual eligible to enroll in a dental benefits plan, information on services and benefits provided by qualified companies participating under chapter 89.

(Added Pub. L. 108–496, § 2, Dec. 23, 2004, 118 Stat. 4004.)
