§ 7255. Short title
This subpart may be cited as the “Star Schools Act”.
(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5471, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1829.)

Prior ProvisionsProvisions naming former part B (§ 6891 et seq.) of this subchapter as the “Star Schools Act” were contained in section 6891 of this title, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–110.
