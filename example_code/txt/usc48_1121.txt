§ 2004. Administrative matters(a) Administrative supportThe Secretary of the Interior shall provide all necessary administrative support to accomplish the requirements of sections 2002 and 2003 of this title.


(b) Authorization of appropriationsThere are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.


(Pub. L. 99–239, title III, § 304, Jan. 14, 1986, 99 Stat. 1837.)

Codification
Section was formerly set out as a note under section 1681 of this title.
