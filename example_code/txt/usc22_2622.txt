§ 5321. Short title
This subchapter may be cited as the “International Debt Management Act of 1988”.
(Pub. L. 100–418, title III, § 3101, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1375.)


References in TextThis subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this subtitle”, meaning subtitle B (§§ 3101–3123) of title III of Pub. L. 100–418, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1375, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of subtitle B to the Code, see Tables.
