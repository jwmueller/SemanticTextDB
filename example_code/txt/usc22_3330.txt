§ 9011. Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of the Convention and this chapter.
(Pub. L. 100–300, § 12, Apr. 29, 1988, 102 Stat. 442.)


References in TextThis chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act” meaning Pub. L. 100–300, Apr. 29, 1988, 102 Stat. 437, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note under section 9001 of this title and Tables.

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 11610 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare.
