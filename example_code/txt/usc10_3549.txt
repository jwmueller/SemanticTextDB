“SEC. 4420. COMMISSARY AND EXCHANGE PRIVILEGES.“The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations to authorize a person who involuntarily ceases to be a member of the Selected Reserve during the force reduction transition period to continue to use commissary and exchange stores in the same manner as a member of the Selected Reserve for a period of two years beginning on the later of—“(1) the date on which that person ceases to be a member of the Selected Reserve; or

“(2) the date of the enactment of this Act [Oct. 23, 1992].
