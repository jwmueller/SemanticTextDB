§ 2231. Reference to chapter 1803
Provisions of law relating to facilities for reserve components are set forth in chapter 1803 of this title (beginning with section 18231).
(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1664(b)(11), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 3011.)

Prior ProvisionsPrior sections 2231 to 2239 were renumbered sections 18231 to 18239 of this title, respectively.

Effective DateSection effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337, set out as a note under section 10001 of this title.
