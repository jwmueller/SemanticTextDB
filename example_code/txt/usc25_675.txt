§ 500e. Revolving fund; moneys not to be covered into Treasury
Except as herein otherwise specially provided, none of the moneys collected or received by the Secretary of the Interior in his administration of this subchapter shall be paid into the Treas­ury, but all such moneys shall constitute a revolving fund to be administered by the Secretary of the Interior for the purposes of this subchapter.
(Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 6, 50 Stat. 900.)


References in TextHerein, referred to in text, means act Sept. 1, 1937, which comprises this subchapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 250e of Title 48, Territories and Insular Possessions.
