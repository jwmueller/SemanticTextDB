§ 410ggg–2. World War II home front study
The Secretary shall conduct a theme study of the World War II home front to determine whether other sites in the United States meet the criteria for potential inclusion in the National Park System in accordance with section 1a–5 of this title.
(Pub. L. 106–352, § 4, Oct. 24, 2000, 114 Stat. 1372.)
