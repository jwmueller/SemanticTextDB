§ 14. Contracts not affected
This title shall not apply to contracts made prior to January 1, 1926.
(July 30, 1947, ch. 392, 61 Stat. 674.)

DerivationAct Feb. 12, 1925, ch. 213, § 15, 43 Stat. 886.

Prior ProvisionsAct Feb. 12, 1925, ch. 213, § 14, 43 Stat. 886, former provisions of section 14 of this title relating to “short title” is not now covered.
