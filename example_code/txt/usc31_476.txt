§ 6906. Funding
For each of fiscal years 2008 through 2014—(1) each county or other eligible unit of local government shall be entitled to payment under this chapter; and

(2) sums shall be made available to the Secretary of the Interior for obligation or expenditure in accordance with this chapter.

(Pub. L. 97–258, Sept. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1035; Pub. L. 110–343, div. C, title VI, § 601(c)(1), Oct. 3, 2008, 122 Stat. 3911; Pub. L. 112–141, div. F, title I, § 100111, July 6, 2012, 126 Stat. 906; Pub. L. 113–79, title XII, § 12312, Feb. 7, 2014, 128 Stat. 992.)
