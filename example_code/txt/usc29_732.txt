§ 2891. Operating plan(a) In generalThe provisions of the contract between the Secretary and an entity selected to operate a Job Corps center shall, at a minimum, serve as an operating plan for the Job Corps center.


(b) Additional informationThe Secretary may require the operator, in order to remain eligible to operate the Job Corps center, to submit such additional information as the Secretary may require, which shall be considered part of the operating plan.


(c) AvailabilityThe Secretary shall make the operating plan described in subsections (a) and (b) of this section, excluding any proprietary information, available to the public.


(Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 151, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1013.)
