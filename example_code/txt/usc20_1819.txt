§ 7217d. Definitions
In this part:(1) Local educational agencyThe term “local educational agency” means a local educational agency or a consortium of such agencies.


(2) Public schoolThe term “public school” means a public elementary school or a public secondary school.


(3) School-age populationThe term “school-age population” means the population aged 5 through 17.


(4) StateThe term “State” means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.


(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5145, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1788.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 5145 of Pub. L. 89–10 was classified to section 3224a of this title, prior to the general amendment of Pub. L. 89–10 by Pub. L. 103–382.
