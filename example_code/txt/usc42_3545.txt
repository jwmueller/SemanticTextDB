“SEC. 311. TRANSFER OF FUNDS FROM OTHER FEDERAL AGENCIES.“(a) In General.—Subject to subsection (c), for purposes of this Act [probably means this title], the Commission may accept transfers of funds from other Federal agencies.

“(b) Transfers.—Any Federal agency authorized to carry out an activity that is within the authority of the Commission may transfer to the Commission any appropriated funds for the activity.

“(c) Treatment.—Any funds transferred to the Commission under this subsection—“(1) shall remain available until expended; and

“(2) may, to the extent necessary to carry out this Act [probably means this title], be transferred to, and merged with, the amounts made available by appropriations Acts for the Commission by the Federal Cochairperson.”
