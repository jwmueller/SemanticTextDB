§ 1826. Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.
(Pub. L. 95–157, § 6, Nov. 8, 1977, 91 Stat. 1267.)

Codification
Section was formerly classified to section 1694e of this title.

Effective DateFor effective date of this section, see section 1825 of this title and Effective Date of Constitution note thereunder.
