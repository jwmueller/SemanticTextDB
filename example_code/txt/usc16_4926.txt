§ 6204. Administration of recreation residence program
The Secretary shall ensure, to the maximum extent practicable, that the basis and procedure for calculating cabin user fees results in a fee for an authorization that reflects, in accordance with this chapter—(1) the market value of a lot; and

(2) regional and local economic influences.

(Pub. L. 106–291, title VI, § 605, Oct. 11, 2000, 114 Stat. 1015.)
