§ 674. Short title
This Act may be cited as the “Agricultural Marketing Agreement Act of 1937”.
(June 3, 1937, ch. 296, § 6, 50 Stat. 249.)


References in TextThis Act, referred to in text, is act June 3, 1937, ch. 296, 50 Stat. 246, as amended, which among other things enacted this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
