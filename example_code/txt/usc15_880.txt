§ 714l. Interest of Members of Congress
The provisions of section 6306(a) of title 41 shall apply to all contracts or agreements of the Corporation, except contracts or agreements of a kind which the Corporation may enter into with farmers participating in a program of the Corporation.
(June 29, 1948, ch. 704, § 14, 62 Stat. 1074.)

Codification
In text, “section 6306(a) of title 41” substituted for “section 1 of the Act of February 27, 1877, as amended (41 U.S.C., 1940 edition, 22)” on authority of Pub. L. 111–350, § 6(c), Jan. 4, 2011, 124 Stat. 3854, which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.


Exceptions From Transfer of Functions
For exception of functions of corporations of Department of Agriculture from transfer of functions to Secretary of Agriculture by Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1953, see Exceptions From Transfer of Functions note set out under section 712a of this title.
