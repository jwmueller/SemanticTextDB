§ 1201. Omitted
Codification
Section, acts Dec. 17, 1942, ch. 739, § 1, 56 Stat. 1053; Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(32), 60 Stat. 868; July 3, 1952, ch. 570, § 1(a)(1), 66 Stat. 330, which related to acquisition and operation of buildings and facilities by the Secretary of the Navy, terminated Aug. 1, 1953.

Continuation of Provisions Until August 1, 1953Act July 3, 1952, ch. 570, § 6, 66 Stat. 334, repealed act Apr. 14, 1952, ch. 204, 66 Stat. 54 as amended by acts May 28, 1952, ch. 339, 66 Stat. 96; June 14, 1952, ch. 437, 66 Stat. 137; June 30, 1952, ch. 526, 66 Stat. 296, which had continued the effectiveness of the provisions of this section until July 3, 1952. Section 1(a) of act July 3, 1952, as amended by acts Mar. 31, 1953, ch. 13, § 1, 67 Stat. 18 and June 30, 1953, ch. 172, 67 Stat. 132, extended the time limitation on the effectiveness of the provisions of this section from Apr. 1, 1953 to Aug. 1, 1953.
