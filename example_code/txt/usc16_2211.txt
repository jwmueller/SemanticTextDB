§ 460hhh. Definitions
As used in this subchapter:(1) National Forest landsThe term “National Forest lands” means lands included in the National Forest System (as defined in section 1609(a) of this title).


(2) Recreation AreaThe term “Recreation Area” means the Spring Mountains National Recreation Area established by this subchapter.


(3) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.


(Pub. L. 103–63, § 2, Aug. 4, 1993, 107 Stat. 297.)

Short TitlePub. L. 103–63, § 1, Aug. 4, 1993, 107 Stat. 297, provided that: “This Act [enacting this subchapter] may be cited as the ‘Spring Mountains National Recreation Area Act’.”
