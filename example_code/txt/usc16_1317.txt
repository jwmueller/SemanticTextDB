§ 428p–2. Administration of Fort Donelson National Battlefield
The Secretary of the Interior shall administer the Fort Donelson National Battlefield in accordance with sections 428p to 428p–2 of this title and the laws generally applicable to units of the National Park System, including sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title and sections 461 to 467 of this title.
(Pub. L. 108–367, § 4, Oct. 25, 2004, 118 Stat. 1744.)
