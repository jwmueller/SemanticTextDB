§ 390h–12p. Tooele Wastewater Treatment and Reuse Project(a) AuthorizationThe Secretary, in cooperation with Tooele City, Utah, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and construction of the Tooele Wastewater Treatment and Reuse Project.


(b) Cost shareThe Federal share of the cost of a project described in subsection (a) of this section shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost.


(c) LimitationThe Secretary shall not provide funds for the operation or maintenance of a project described in subsection (a) of this section.


(Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1630, as added Pub. L. 104–266, § 2(a)(2), Oct. 9, 1996, 110 Stat. 3294.)
