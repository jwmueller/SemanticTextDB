§ 430ss. Administration, protection, and development
The administration, protection, and development of the Fort Necessity National Battlefield shall be exercised by the Secretary of the Interior in accordance with provisions of sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented.
(Pub. L. 87–134, § 4, Aug. 10, 1961, 75 Stat. 336.)
