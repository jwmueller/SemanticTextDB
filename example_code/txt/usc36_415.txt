§ 40510. Liability(a) Liability of Corporation.—The corporation is liable for the acts of its officers and agents acting within the scope of their authority.

(b) Immunity of Individuals.—A member or private individual is not liable for the obligations of the corporation.

(Pub. L. 105–225, Aug. 12, 1998, 112 Stat. 1335.)
