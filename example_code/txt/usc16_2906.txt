§ 590j. Definitions
In this chapter:(1) Agricultural commodityThe term “agricultural commodity” means—(A) an agricultural commodity; and

(B) any regional or market classification, type, or grade of an agricultural commodity.


(2) Technical assistance(A) In generalThe term “technical assistance” means technical expertise, information, and tools necessary for the conservation of natural resources on land active in agricultural, forestry, or related uses.


(B) InclusionsThe term “technical assistance” includes—(i) technical services provided directly to farmers, ranchers, and other eligible entities, such as conservation planning, technical consultation, and assistance with design and implementation of conservation practices; and

(ii) technical infrastructure, including activities, processes, tools, and agency functions needed to support delivery of technical services, such as technical standards, resource inventories, training, data, technology, monitoring, and effects analyses.
