§ 1079d. Repealed. Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(13), Oct. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 2160

Section, acts May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 5, 70 Stat. 125; July 12, 1960, Pub. L. 86–612, § 2(a), 74 Stat. 371, related to grants to needy ineligible widows.

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465, set out as an Effective Date note under section 3901 of this title.

Effective Date of 1960 AmendmentPub. L. 86–612, § 2(b), July 12, 1960, 74 Stat. 371, which provided that the amendment made to this section by section 2(a) of Pub. L. 86–612 take effect on the first day of the first month which begins more than thirty days after July 12, 1960, was repealed by Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(15), Oct. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 2160.
