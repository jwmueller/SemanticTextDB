§ 8084. Officer career field for space
The Secretary of the Air Force shall establish and implement policies and procedures to develop a career field for officers in the Air Force with technical competence in space-related matters to have the capability to—(1) develop space doctrine and concepts of space operations;

(2) develop space systems; and

(3) operate space systems.

(Added Pub. L. 107–107, div. A, title IX, § 912(a), Dec. 28, 2001, 115 Stat. 1196; amended Pub. L. 108–375, div. A, title X, § 1084(d)(32), Oct. 28, 2004, 118 Stat. 2063.)

Amendments2004—Pub. L. 108–375 substituted “capability” for “capabilty”.
