“SEC. 8. TERMINATION OF STATE PARTICIPATION.“Participation by a State in the pilot program may be terminated—“(1) by the Secretary, if the Secretary—“(A) finds that the State has violated any of the terms of the application of the State approved by the Secretary under section 5; and

“(B) provides to the State written notice of the termination by not later than the date that is 30 days before the date of termination; or


“(2) by the State, by providing written notice to the Secretary by not later than the date that is 30 days before the termination date.
