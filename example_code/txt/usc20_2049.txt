§ 7841. Purposes
The purposes of this part are—(1) to improve teaching and learning by encouraging greater cross-program coordination, planning, and service delivery;

(2) to provide greater flexibility to State and local authorities through consolidated plans, applications, and reporting; and

(3) to enhance the integration of programs under this chapter with State and local programs.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9301, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1968.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 9301 of Pub. L. 89–10 was classified to section 7931 of this title, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–110.
