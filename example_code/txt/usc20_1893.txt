§ 7261. Short title
This subpart may be cited as the “Carol M. White Physical Education Program”.
(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5501, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1841.)

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 7261, Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5301, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3701, set forth short title of the School Dropout Assistance Act, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–110.
