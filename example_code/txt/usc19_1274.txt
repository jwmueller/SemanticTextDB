“SEC. 324. ARTICLES EXEMPT FROM RELIEF.“The President may not provide import relief under this subtitle with respect to any article if—“(1) the article has been subject to import relief under this subtitle after the date on which the Agreement enters into force [Aug. 1, 2006]; or

“(2) the article is subject to import relief under chapter 1 of title II of the Trade Act of 1974 (19 U.S.C. 2251 et seq.).
