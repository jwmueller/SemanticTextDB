Section, Pub. L. 92–522, title III, § 304, as added Pub. L. 102–523, § 2(a), Oct. 26, 1992, 106 Stat. 3428, related to reviews, reports, and recommendations by Secretary of Commerce.

Effective Date of RepealFor effective date of repeal, see section 8 of Pub. L. 105–42, set out as an Effective Date of 1997 Amendment note under section 1362 of this title.
