§ 6405. Emergency assistance
The Administrator may make grants to any State, local, or territorial government agency with jurisdiction over coral reefs for emergencies to address unforeseen or disaster-related circumstance 11 So in original. pertaining to coral reefs or coral reef ecosystems.
(Pub. L. 106–562, title II, § 206, Dec. 23, 2000, 114 Stat. 2804.)
