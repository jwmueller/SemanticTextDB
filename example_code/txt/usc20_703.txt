§ 1099e. Transferred
Codification
Section, Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 499A, as added Pub. L. 110–84, title VIII, § 802, Sept. 27, 2007, 121 Stat. 817, which related to investment in historically Black colleges and universities and other minority-serving institutions, was transferred to section 1067q of this title.
