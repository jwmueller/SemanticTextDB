§ 410mm–3. Authorization of appropriations(a) Not more than $800,000 are authorized to be appropriated for development of the park.

(b) Not more than $200,000 are authorized to be appropriated for acquisition of lands and interests in land within the park.

(Pub. L. 99–565, § 5, Oct. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 3183.)
