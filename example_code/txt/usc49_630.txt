“SEC. 7. DEFINITIONS.“As used in this Act, the following definitions apply:“(1) Child restraint.—The term ‘child restraint’ means any product designed to provide restraint to a child (including booster seats and other products used with a lap and shoulder belt assembly) that meets applicable Federal motor vehicle safety standards prescribed by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.

“(2) Production year.—The term ‘production year’ means the 12-month period between September 1 of a year and August 31 of the following year.

“(3) Passenger motor vehicle.—The term ‘passenger motor vehicle’ has the meaning given that term in [former] section 405(f)(5) of title 23, United States Code.
