§ 428d–3. Administration, protection, and development
The administration, protection, and development of the lands authorized to be added to the Fort Donelson National Battlefield by sections 428d–1 and 428d–2 shall be exercised under the direction of the Secretary of the Interior by the National Park Service, subject to the provisions of sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended.
(Aug. 30, 1937, ch. 888, § 3, 50 Stat. 883; Pub. L. 86–738, § 4, Sept. 8, 1960, 74 Stat. 876.)


Change of Name
“Fort Donelson National Battlefield” substituted in text for “Fort Donelson National Military Park” in view of redesignation of Fort Donelson National Military Park as Fort Donelson National Battlefield by Pub. L. 86–738. See section 428n of this title.
