§ 4413. Executive Board(a) CompositionThe Board shall have an Executive Board composed of—(1) the chairman of the Board;

(2) the vice chairman of the Board;

(3) the secretary of the Board;

(4) the treasurer of the Board; and

(5) an at-large member of the Board elected by the Board at its initial meeting.


(b) VacanciesIn the case of any vacancy which occurs in the position of at-large member before the expiration of such member’s term, the Board shall elect a replacement to complete that term.


(c) MeetingsThe Executive Board shall hold not more than 4 regular meetings per calendar year. Special meetings may be held upon the call of the chairman or 3 members of the Executive Board.


(d) QuorumA majority of the Executive Board shall constitute a quorum.


(e) PowersThe Executive Board may hold and use all the powers of the Board, subject to the approval of the Board.


(Pub. L. 99–498, title XV, § 1506, Oct. 17, 1986, 100 Stat. 1603.)
