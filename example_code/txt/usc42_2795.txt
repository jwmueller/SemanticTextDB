§ 2000h–5. Authorization of appropriations
There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.
(Pub. L. 88–352, title XI, § 1105, July 2, 1964, 78 Stat. 268.)


References in TextThis Act, referred to in text, is Pub. L. 88–352, July 2, 1964, 78 Stat. 241, as amended, known as the Civil Rights Act of 1964, which is classified principally to subchapters II to IX of this chapter (§ 2000a et seq.). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 2000a of this title and Tables.
