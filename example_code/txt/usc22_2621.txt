§ 5306. Definitions
As used in this subchapter:(1) SecretaryThe term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Treasury.


(2) BoardThe term “Board” means the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System.


(Pub. L. 100–418, title III, § 3006, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1375.)
