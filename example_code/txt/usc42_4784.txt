§ 6003. Repealed. Pub. L. 106–402, title IV, § 401(a), Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1737

A prior section 6003, Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 104, as added Pub. L. 94–103, title I, § 125, Oct. 4, 1975, 89 Stat. 498, set forth provisions relating to State control of operations, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by Pub. L. 98–527.
