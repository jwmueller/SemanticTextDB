§ 460hhh–6. Withdrawal(a) In generalSubject to valid existing rights and except for lands described in subsection (b) of this section, all Federal lands within the Recreation Area are withdrawn from—(1) all forms of entry, appropriation, or disposal under the public land laws;

(2) location, entry, and patent under the mining laws; and

(3) operation under the mineral leasing and geothermal leasing laws.


(b) ExceptionThe lands referred to in subsection (a) of this section are described as follows:
W½E½ and W½, Sec. 27, T23S, R58E, Mt. Diablo Meridian.


(Pub. L. 103–63, § 8, Aug. 4, 1993, 107 Stat. 300.)
