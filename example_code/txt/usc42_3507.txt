§ 3058a. Authorization of appropriations(a) Ombudsman programThere are authorized to be appropriated to carry out subpart II of this part, such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2007, and such sums as may be necessary for subsequent fiscal years.


(b) Prevention of elder abuse, neglect, and exploitationThere are authorized to be appropriated to carry out subpart III of this part, such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2007, and such sums as may be necessary for subsequent fiscal years.


(c) Legal assistance development programThere are authorized to be appropriated to carry out subpart IV of this part, such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2007, and such sums as may be necessary for subsequent fiscal years.


(Pub. L. 89–73, title VII, § 702, as added Pub. L. 102–375, title VII, § 701, Sept. 30, 1992, 106 Stat. 1271; amended Pub. L. 106–501, title VII, § 701, Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2289; Pub. L. 109–365, title VII, § 701, Oct. 17, 2006, 120 Stat. 2591.)
