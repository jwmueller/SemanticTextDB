“SEC. 6. IMPROVING RECOVERY OF IMPROPER PAYMENTS.“(a) Definition.—In this section, the term ‘recovery audit’ means a recovery audit described under section 2(h) of the Improper Payments Elimination and Recovery Act of 2010 [Pub. L. 111–204] (31 U.S.C. 3301 note).

“(b) Review.—The Director of the Office of Management and Budget shall determine—“(1) current and historical rates and amounts of recovery of improper payments (or, in cases in which improper payments are identified solely on the basis of a sample, recovery rates and amounts estimated on the basis of the applicable sample), including a list of agency recovery audit contract programs and specific information of amounts and payments recovered by recovery audit contractors; and

“(2) targets for recovering improper payments, including specific information on amounts and payments recovered by recovery audit contractors.”
