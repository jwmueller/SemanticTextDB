§ 3007. Publication of Harmonized Tariff Schedule(a) In generalThe Commission shall compile and publish, at appropriate intervals, and keep up to date the Harmonized Tariff Schedule and related information in the form of printed copy; and, if, in its judgment, such format would serve the public interest and convenience—(1) in the form of microfilm images; or

(2) in the form of electronic media.


(b) ContentPublications under subsection (a) of this section, in whatever format, shall contain—(1) the then current Harmonized Tariff Schedule;

(2) statistical annotations and related statistical information formulated under section 1484(f) of this title; and

(3) such other matters as the Commission considers to be necessary or appropriate to carry out the purposes enumerated in the Preamble to the Convention.


(Pub. L. 100–418, title I, § 1207, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1151; Pub. L. 104–295, § 21(e)(10), Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3531.)


References in TextThe Harmonized Tariff Schedule, referred to in subsecs. (a) and (b)(1), is not set out in the Code. See Publication of Harmonized Tariff Schedule note set out under section 1202 of this title.

Amendments1996—Subsec. (b)(2). Pub. L. 104–295 substituted “1484(f)” for “1484(e)”.
