§ 7425. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.


(b) Limitation on expenditures for administrative expensesFunds appropriated to carry out this subchapter may not be expended for the payment of expenses incurred by a board to administer an order.


(Pub. L. 104–127, title V, § 526, Apr. 4, 1996, 110 Stat. 1048.)
