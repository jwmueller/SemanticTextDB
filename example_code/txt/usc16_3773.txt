§ 1010. Data
The Secretary shall collect and maintain data on a national and State by State basis concerning—(1) expenditures for the individual flood control and conservation measures for which assistance is provided under this chapter; and

(2) the expected flood control or environmental (including soil erosion) benefits that will result from the implementation of such measures.

(Aug. 4, 1954, ch. 656, § 13, as added Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1463, Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 3615.)
