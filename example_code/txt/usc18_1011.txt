§ 3110. Property defined—(Rule)See Federal Rules of Criminal ProcedureTerm “property” as used in Rule 41 includes documents, books, papers and any other tangible objects, Rule 41(g).


(June 25, 1948, ch. 645, 62 Stat. 820.)


References in TextRule 41(g), referred to in text, was redesignated 41(h) by 1972 amendment eff. Oct. 1, 1972.
