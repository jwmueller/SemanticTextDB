§ 1300k–1. Definitions
For purposes of this subchapter—(1) the term “Bands” means the Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians and the Little River Band of Ottawa Indians;

(2) the term “member” means those individuals enrolled in the Bands pursuant to section 1300k–5 of this title; and

(3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(Pub. L. 103–324, § 3, Sept. 21, 1994, 108 Stat. 2157.)
