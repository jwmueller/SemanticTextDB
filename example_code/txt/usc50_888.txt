§ 2791. Laboratory-directed research and development programs(a) AuthorityGovernment-owned, contractor-operated laboratories that are funded out of funds available to the Department of Energy for national security programs are authorized to carry out laboratory-directed research and development.


(b) RegulationsThe Secretary of Energy shall prescribe regulations for the conduct of laboratory-directed research and development at such laboratories.


(c) FundingOf the funds provided by the Department of Energy to such laboratories for national security activities, the Secretary shall provide a specific amount, not to exceed 6 percent of such funds, to be used by such laboratories for laboratory-directed research and development.


(d) “Laboratory-directed research and development” definedFor purposes of this section, the term “laboratory-directed research and development” means research and development work of a creative and innovative nature which, under the regulations prescribed pursuant to subsection (b), is selected by the director of a laboratory for the purpose of maintaining the vitality of the laboratory in defense-related scientific disciplines.
