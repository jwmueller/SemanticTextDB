§ 6111. Authorization of appropriations(a) In generalThere are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.


(b) Administrative expensesThe funds so appropriated shall not be available for payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Council in administering any provision of an order issued under this chapter.


(Pub. L. 101–624, title XIX, § 1932, Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 3865.)
