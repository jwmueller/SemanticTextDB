§ 9041. Judicial review(a) Review of agency action by the CommissionAny agency action by the Commission made under the provisions of this chapter shall be subject to review by the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit upon petition filed in such court within 30 days after the agency action by the Commission for which review is sought.


(b) Review proceduresThe provisions of chapter 7 of title 5, United States Code, apply to judicial review of any agency action, as defined in section 551(13) of title 5, United States Code, by the Commission.


(Added Pub. L. 93–443, title IV, § 408(c), Oct. 15, 1974, 88 Stat. 1302.)
