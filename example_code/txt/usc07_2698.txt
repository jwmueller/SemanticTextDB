§ 7917. Relation to remaining payment authority under production flexibility contracts(a) Termination of superseded payment authorityNotwithstanding section 7213(a)(7) of this title or any other provision of law, the Secretary shall not make payments for fiscal year 2002 after May 13, 2002, under a production flexibility contract entered into under section 7211 of this title unless requested by the producer that is a party to the contract.


(b) Contract payments made before enactmentIf a producer receives all or any portion of the payment authorized for fiscal year 2002 under a production flexibility contract, the Secretary shall reduce the amount of the direct payment otherwise due the producer for the 2002 crop year under section 7913 of this title by the amount of the fiscal year 2002 payment received by the producer under the production flexibility contract.


(Pub. L. 107–171, title I, § 1107, May 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 154.)
