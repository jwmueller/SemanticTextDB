§ 7316. Lifeboatmen
To qualify for an endorsement as lifeboatman, an applicant must provide satisfactory proof that the applicant—(1) has the service or training required by regulation;

(2) is qualified professionally as demonstrated by examination; and

(3) is qualified professionally by actual demonstration.

(Pub. L. 98–89, Aug. 26, 1983, 97 Stat. 544.)
