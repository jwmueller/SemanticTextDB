§ 7314. Reports(a) Report on initial reviewNot later than June 1, 1998, the Commission shall submit to the President, the Committee on Agriculture of the House of Representatives, and the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry of the Senate a report containing the results of the initial review conducted under section 7313(a) of this title.


(b) Report on subsequent reviewNot later than January 1, 2001, the Commission shall submit to the President and the congressional committees specified in subsection (a) a report containing the results of the subsequent review conducted under section 7313(b) of this title.


(Pub. L. 104–127, title I, § 184, Apr. 4, 1996, 110 Stat. 940.)
