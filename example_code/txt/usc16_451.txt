§ 217b. Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historical Park(a) DesignationThe Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historic Site in the State of Kentucky shall be known and designated as the “Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historical Park”.


(b) ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historic Site shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historical Park”.


(Pub. L. 111–11, title VII, § 7114, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1202.)
