“SEC. 202. REQUIREMENT FOR IMPROVEMENT IN SERVICES FOR VETERANS.“A proposed agreement authorized by section 201 that is entered into by the head of a Department of Veterans Affairs medical facility may take effect only if the Under Secretary for Health of the Department of Veterans Affairs finds, and certifies to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, that implementation of the agreement—“(1) will result in the improvement of services to eligible veterans at that facility; and

“(2) will not result in the denial of, or a delay in providing, access to care for any veteran at that facility.
