§ 410iii–5. Administration
The Secretary shall administer the Park in accordance with this subchapter and the provisions of law generally applicable to units of the National Park System, including—(1) sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title; and

(2) sections 461 to 467 of this title.

(Pub. L. 107–373, § 7, Dec. 19, 2002, 116 Stat. 3106.)
